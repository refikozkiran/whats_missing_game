How to Play

1
Memorize the items
A set of emoji items appears on screen. Study them carefully — you have limited time!
2
The screen flashes
The screen blinks and one item disappears. Pay close attention!
3
Find what's missing
Three options appear below. Tap the one that was removed.
4
Level up!
Every correct answer levels you up. Higher levels = more items + less time to memorize.
5
Don't lose all lives
You have 3 lives in Classic mode. 3 wrong answers and it's game over!
💡 Pro Tips
• Focus on the shape and color of each emoji
• At higher levels, scan left-to-right like reading
• In Time Attack, speed matters more than accuracy
• Sudden Death rewards calm, careful thinking
